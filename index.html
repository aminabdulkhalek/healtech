Elie D. Al-Chaer, PhD, JD

CEO & Co-founder.

Learn More

Dr. Elie D. Al-Chaer is an accomplished entrepreneur, dynamic leader, and a savvy attorney and counselor. He is the founder and CEO of the Law Offices of Elie D. AlChaer, a highly respected law firm based in Dallas, Texas. A remarkable blend of scientist, mathematician, and legal mastermind with a fervent passion for the intricate workings of the brain, Elie has an insatiable appetite for cutting-edge research and groundbreaking innovation. He has held numerous distinguished academic positions and served on advisory boards of several Fortune 500 companies, earning a reputation as a trailblazer and visionary.
Dr. Al-Chaer is a transformational leader who combines his unparalleled expertise with a unique perspective to pave the way for a brighter future, where science, medicine, and law intersect in unprecedented ways. With his unwavering commitment to excellence, he is the driving force behind a new era of progress, change, and success.

Ayman Kayssi, PHD

Co-founder

Learn more

Dr Ayman Kayssi is a professor at the American University of Beirut (AUB) and served as co-founder and CTO of several technology startups. He has more than 35 years of experience in hardware, software, cybersecurity, privacy, telecom and internet technologies, blockchain, machine learning, and AR/XR. Ayman obtained his PhD from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 1993 and his Bachelor of Engineering from the American University of Beirut in 1987. He was chairman of the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at AUB, and associate dean of its Maroun Semaan Faculty of Engineering and Architecture. He is a senior member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers.

Imad H. Elhajj, PHD

Co-founder

Learn more

Imad H. Elhajj is a Professor with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and is the Associate Dean for Academic Transformation at the Maroun Semaan Faculty of Engineering and Architecture at the American University of Beirut. In 2014, he co-founded SAUGO 360, the first startup to be incubated at AUB, and in 2017 he co-founded the Humanitarian Engineering Initiative at AUB. Dr. Elhajj is the past chair of IEEE Lebanon Section and serves as an ABET program evaluator since 2013. His research interests are at the intersection of robotics, networking, machine learning and human machine interfacing with applications in health and environment. Dr. Elhajj is recipient of the IEEE Outstanding Branch Counselor and Advisor Award (2019), the Teaching Excellence Award at the American University of Beirut (2011), the Kamal Salibi Academic Freedom Award (2014), and the most Outstanding Graduate Student Award from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Michigan State University (2001).

Hussain Isma'eel, MD

Consultant

Learn more

Dr. Hussain Ismaeel trained in cardiology at AUB, and subspecialized in cardiac imaging at Harbor-UCLA and Cleveland Clinic. He has more than 10 years of experience in healthcare performance improvement and holds Lean Six Sigma in healthcare from Emory University. his passion to AI led to patents in the field & numerous publications in using AI for diagnostic and predictive analysis in cardiovascular diseases. He is also certified in the Ethics & Governance of AI in Healthcare. His vision is to completely digitize the clinical encounter to optimize diagnosis and management of patients and improve outcomes
