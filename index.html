Ayman Kayssi, PHD

Co-founder

Learn more

Dr. Ayman Kayssi is a professor at the American University of Beirut (AUB) with 35+ years of experience in technology and entrepreneurship. He co-founded and served as CTO for several startups in hardware, software, cybersecurity, privacy, telecom, internet technologies, blockchain, machine learning, and AR/XR. Imad H. Elhajj, PHD

Co-founder

Learn more

Imad H. Elhajj is an Associate Dean and Professor at the American University of Beirut's Maroun Semaan Faculty of Engineering and Architecture.
